About

FramerFlow is a simple editor to help you creating beautiful prototypes. Prototyping is a sort of proactive exploration to undiscovered things. That is one of the reasons why, we believe, prototyping has a tremendous fun. We want to make it more enjoyable and manageable. That's why we have created FramerFlow.

We have some plans to bring cutting-edge exploration tools in coming releases. Please keep eyes on FramerFlow.

Happy prototyping.
